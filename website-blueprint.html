Positioning & ground rules

One-liner: DhwaNeer is a turnkey commercial water-treatment project & engineering company (WTP, RO, softening, pumping, AMC) for restaurants, hotels, corporate & industrial sites — one accountable, system-driven team from water report to lifelong AMC, delivered via the WATERS method.

  • Audience: owners / chefs / facility heads / MEP & kitchen consultants planning or fixing water infrastructure.
  • Proof bank: 120+ restaurant · 20+ hotel · 50+ corporate references (190+ total); Bastian, Burma Burma, Yauatcha, Fairmont, Westin, Marriott, KKR, Barclays…
  • Authorized platforms: Pentair Everpure (90+ yrs) + ClairHydra. Components: Astral · Sintex · John Guest.
  • Guardrail: never publish the ₹35L project figure (anchors low). Use reference counts instead.
  • Guardrail: product imagery must not read “distributor” — projects & process lead, products support.

Design system — verdict: keep, refine

Navy #09244F Water blue #4897C3 Indigo #403594 · restrained Green #4F8309 · success only WhatsApp CTA only
  • Type: Barlow + Barlow Condensed — keep as-is.
  • Palette: no overhaul. Indigo stays secondary/gradient-only (differentiator); if a purer “water” feel is ever wanted, shift gradients toward teal — 1-hour tweak, not a redesign.
  • Rhythm: homepage stays dark-hero/alternating; inner pages lean light for long reading.
  • Cards: one radius/shadow scale everywhere; WATERS letter-badges are the recurring brand motif.
  • Biggest design upgrade isn’t color — it’s real photography replacing catalog renders.
  • Respect prefers-reduced-motion; marquee + walkthrough already do.
§9

SEO, performance & tracking

SEO

  • 301s: water-heater + both old commercial pages → Solutions.
  • Schema: LocalBusiness LIVE + add FAQPage, Service, BreadcrumbList on inner pages.
  • Unique title/meta per page; domain pages target “water treatment for <segment> india / mumbai”.
  • Alt text = product/project names (already practiced).
  • XML sitemap + robots once inner pages ship.

Performance & tracking

  • GA4 + Meta pixel; conversion = wa.me click (per-CTA labels), call clicks.
  • Compress/normalize images (done for products; repeat for photos), lazy-load below fold, preload hero font/CSS.
  • Keep pages self-contained & light — current approach is fast; hold that line as pages multiply.
  • UTM discipline on all campaigns; WhatsApp number consistent everywhere.
