Engineering That Pays For Itself

Where our projects save you money

Every proposal connects the site problem, the selected system, the execution scope and the outcome — space, cost and water, accounted for.

Save Space

Plants designed around real site constraints

Rooftop venues, heritage buildings and packed back-of-house areas rarely offer ideal plant rooms. Compact skids, stacked filtration banks and wall-mounted manifolds recover usable, revenue-earning area.

In practice: space-constrained installs delivered for rooftop and premium venues like Bastian At The Top, Dadar.

Save Money

Equipment protected, breakdowns prevented

Scale is the silent killer of combi ovens, dishwashers, ice machines and boilers. Correct softening and filtration means fewer breakdowns, longer equipment life and lower service bills — year after year.

In practice: multi-city groups like Burma Burma standardize water quality across outlets to protect identical kitchen lines.

Protect your equipment
Cut Water Waste

Right-sized systems, not oversold ones

Systems sized to measured peak demand — with high-recovery RO, reject-water reuse planning and demand-matched pumping — so you treat exactly what you need and waste less of what you pay for.

In practice: every DhwaNeer proposal is built from your water analysis and peak demand — not a catalogue page.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Straight answers on scope, brands, water testing and support — the questions facility owners and consultants ask us most.

Do you handle the entire project, or just supply equipment?

We are turnkey. We handle water analysis, system design, supply, installation, plumbing and electrical coordination, commissioning and ongoing AMC — single-point responsibility across the whole project.

Which brands and components do you use?

We build on authorized Pentair Everpure and ClairHydra platforms, integrated with genuine components such as Astral pipes, Sintex tanks and John Guest fittings.

Do you test our water first?

Yes. Every project starts with a lab water report — for example from Equinox — so the system is right-sized for your actual water rather than a generic spec.

What types of sites do you work with?

Restaurants and foodservice, hotels, corporate and institutional facilities, and industrial utilities.

Do you provide maintenance after installation?

Yes. We provide AMC and scheduled servicing. Our support is system-driven, so it continues regardless of staff changes on either side.

Which cities do you serve?

We are based in Mumbai and execute projects pan-India.
