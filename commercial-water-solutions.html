What We Solve

The water problems we fix

Most sites call us with the same handful of problems. Each one has a root cause in the water — and an engineered fix.

Hard water & scaling

Scale kills combi ovens, dishwashers and boilers, and drives up energy and service bills.

High TDS & brackish borewell water

Poor taste, white residue and water that is unsafe or unfit for everyday use.

Sediment & turbidity

Grit and cloudiness clog equipment, foul membranes and shorten filter life.

Microbial contamination

Bacteria and pathogens make water unsafe for drinking and food preparation.

Low & uneven pressure

Weak or fluctuating pressure means inconsistent supply across floors and outlets.

Boiler & cooling-tower scale

Scale and corrosion rob efficiency, raise fuel costs and cut equipment life.

Questions, Answered

Water solutions FAQ

The things buyers ask before choosing a water treatment partner.

Do you design the whole system, or just supply products?

We design the whole system. DhwaNeer is a turnkey engineering company, not a distributor — we analyse your water, design the treatment train, supply it, install and commission it, and maintain it. You deal with one accountable team from start to finish.

Which solution do I need for hard water or high TDS?

Hard water is usually solved with softening, while high TDS or brackish water needs reverse osmosis — often with pre-filtration ahead of both. We confirm the right combination from a lab water report before we design anything.

Do you cover installation and plumbing, or only equipment?

We cover installation end to end. Our own team plumbs, pressure-tests, wires and sanitises the system before commissioning, using genuine components such as Astral pipes, Sintex tanks and John Guest fittings.

Do you provide AMC after installation?

Yes. Every system can run on a planned AMC cycle with scheduled service visits and genuine consumables and spares, so performance holds long after handover. It is system-driven, so service continues even if an engineer moves on.

Which brands do you use?

Systems are built on authorized Pentair Everpure and ClairHydra platforms, integrated with genuine components such as Astral pipes, Sintex tanks and John Guest fittings — all chosen to be reliable and serviceable.

Which cities do you serve?

DhwaNeer is based in Ghatkopar, Mumbai and executes turnkey projects pan-India, including multi-site and multi-city water programmes. Share your city and we will plan the site visit accordingly.
