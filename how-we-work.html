Questions, Answered

How-we-work FAQ

The things prospects ask before handing us a project.

How long does a typical project take?

It depends on scope. A single-outlet filtration or softening system can be delivered in a few days once the water report and design are signed off, while a larger multi-system plant or a multi-site rollout runs over several weeks. You get an indicative schedule with the proposal.

Do you handle plumbing and electrical work too?

Yes. Execution is turnkey — our own team delivers, installs, plumbs and pressure-tests the system and wires it up, then sanitises it before commissioning. You deal with one accountable team, not a chain of separate contractors.

Which brands and components do you use?

Systems are built on authorized Pentair Everpure and ClairHydra platforms, integrated with genuine components such as Astral pipes, Sintex tanks and John Guest fittings. Everything is chosen to be reliable and serviceable.

Do you test our water before designing the system?

Always. We start by lab-testing your water — TDS, hardness, pH, iron and microbes — at Equinox and other leading labs, so the design is engineered for your actual water rather than a catalogue assumption.

Do you provide AMC after handover?

Yes. Support runs on a planned AMC cycle — handover, then service at M3, M6 and M9, then renewal — with consumables and spares planned ahead. It is system-driven, so service continues on schedule even if an engineer moves on.

Do you work outside Mumbai?

Yes. DhwaNeer is based in Mumbai and executes turnkey projects pan-India, including multi-site and multi-city water programmes. Share your city and we will plan the site visit accordingly.
